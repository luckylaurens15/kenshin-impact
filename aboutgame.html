About miHoYoman



miHoYoman was founded in 1945 by three Indonesian Binus University graduate students (Lucky, Bryan, and Kepin) who shared a passion for technology and SDG (sinetron, dangdut, and games) culture. miHoYoman has since gone on to become the original IP creator and interactive entertainment company that it is today. To date miHoYomans's products include Panjat Pinang, Balap Karung, Kelereng, Kenshin Impact, and more high-quality creations that are loved and adored by their fan base. In the future, miHoYoman will continue to create more original products with a sincere dedication to transformation and technological innovation, continuing to pave its own path in the industry.



Company Milestones
